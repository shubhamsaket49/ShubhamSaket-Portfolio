Who I Am

I'm a Unity Developer and XR Engineer with 3+ years of experience building immersive VR, MR, and simulation-based applications. I specialize in transforming complex real-world procedures into interactive training experiences using Unity3D, C#, and XR technologies.

My work spans defense, aerospace, industrial safety, and education. I have contributed to VR training systems for the Indian Air Force and HAL, including aircraft maintenance simulations for the LCA Tejas program, helping personnel safely practice critical operational procedures in realistic virtual environments.

Currently, I am building scalable no-code VR/MR platforms that enable organizations to rapidly create and deploy immersive training solutions. I am passionate about developing impactful XR experiences that improve learning outcomes, operational readiness, and user engagement.
